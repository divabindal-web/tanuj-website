The Journey

Built from
conviction,
not capital.

Eight years studying how global capital moves through property markets before deciding to rewire how India transacts real estate entirely.

2000 – 2005
IIM Ahmedabad
Graduated with SEC, FSA, and Asian securities market certifications. Studied capital allocation mechanics and how assets are mispriced when information is opaque.
2005 – 2008
Standard Chartered · Lehman Brothers
Capital markets across Asia Pacific. Witnessed how global institutions priced large-scale assets — and how much value is destroyed by information asymmetry.
2008 – 2013
Nomura Securities · Dubai
Based in UAE, watching NRIs engage with Indian property. Recognised the broken information infrastructure as a solvable, scalable problem. The idea for Square Yards formed here.
2013
Founded Square Yards
Launched from Gurugram with a global mandate from day one. The conviction: Indian real estate was a data and distribution problem wearing a brokerage costume.
2015 – 2020
International Expansion
Scaled to 9 countries — Middle East, Australia, Canada, Southeast Asia. Built the NRI investment corridor competitors believed could not be systematised.
2022 – Present
Profitable at Scale
₹1,400 Cr+ revenue with consistent EBITDA profitability — among the very few Indian startups to achieve significant scale and positive unit economics simultaneously.
